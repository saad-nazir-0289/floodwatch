What this demo does

  • Detects likely water bodies and flood-like regions using color, texture, and morphology.
  • Builds a built-up proxy from brightness, edge density, and structural texture.
  • Creates change maps for before/after imagery using aligned differencing and similarity cues.
  • Reports quick metrics such as water coverage, built-up proxy coverage, changed area, and connected flood regions.
  • Runs without GPUs or paid APIs.
  • Includes built-in demo imagery so users can test the app instantly.
